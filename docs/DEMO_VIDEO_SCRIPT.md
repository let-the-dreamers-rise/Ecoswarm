# EcoSwarm Regen Demo Video Script

Target length: **4 minutes 20 seconds to 4 minutes 50 seconds**

Primary goal: record **one clean take** that feels like a real founder demo and hits the judging criteria without drifting into vague narration.

Core lines:

- `Capital should not move without proof.`
- `EcoSwarm Regen is not a sustainability dashboard. It is a payout operating system for climate programs.`
- `The trust model is the product.`

## What You Need To Do For Maximum Score

Your video needs to prove six things, in this order:

1. there is a real climate-finance problem
2. the product is focused, not broad and messy
3. the workflow is real across operator, verifier, and sponsor
4. Hedera is essential, not decorative
5. the app is deployed and technically real
6. the project can become a pilot-ready venture

If your video does those six things clearly, it gives you the best chance at top scores in:

- `Innovation`
- `Feasibility`
- `Execution`
- `Integration`
- `Pitch`

## Non-Negotiable Demo Rules

- Do **not** introduce yourself first
- Do **not** begin on a blank or loading screen
- Do **not** move the cursor randomly
- Do **not** read every card on screen
- Do **not** claim fake customers or fake traction
- Do **not** say "dashboard" more than once
- Do **not** spend more than 15 seconds on any page without a reason

Instead:

- start immediately on the strongest live screen
- show state changes
- show real Hedera proof
- say short sentences
- sound like a founder, not like a student presenting slides

## Exact Pre-Recording Setup

Before you hit record:

1. Open the live frontend: [ecoswarm-regen-frontend.vercel.app](https://ecoswarm-regen-frontend.vercel.app)
2. Make sure the left sidebar says `Live sync connected`
3. If no cases are visible, click `Start Simulation` in the top-right scenario panel
4. Wait until you see `Demo Mode Active`
5. Wait until at least one case appears in the sidebar and the app looks populated
6. Pick the strongest case card in the left sidebar under `Active shared case`
7. Keep the browser zoom at `100%`
8. Use dark mode only, full-screen browser, no bookmarks bar
