# Pitch Deck Outline

## Slide 1: Title

`EcoSwarm Regen`

Verified milestone payouts for community climate projects on Hedera

Supporting line:

`A synchronized workflow for sponsors, verifiers, and local operators to move climate funding with less trust friction.`

## Slide 2: The Problem

Climate funding breaks down between proof and payout.

- community projects struggle to prove progress in a way sponsors trust
- sponsors do not want to release funds on weak or fragmented evidence
- verifiers operate in separate systems from operators and funders
- most tools stop at reporting instead of helping capital move

Close with:

`The result is slow funding, fragmented trust, and underfunded local climate action.`

## Slide 3: The Wedge

Do not pitch a broad sustainability platform here.

Pitch one launch wedge:

`Milestone-based funding for community restoration and resilience projects.`

Examples already supported in the product:

- reforestation with survival checkpoints
- river cleanup with municipal or CSR releases
- community solar resilience payouts

## Slide 4: The Product

Show the product as a finished system with four connected views:

- `Overview`: live treasury and platform signals
- `Case Room`: one shared payout case for sponsor, verifier, and operator
- `Operations`: intake, release queue, and treasury AI
- `Client Portal`: sponsor commitments and alerts
- `Audit Trail`: Hedera-backed records and proof packets

Core line:

`EcoSwarm turns field proof into a sponsor-ready payout case, ranks release opportunities, and anchors the audit line on Hedera.`

## Slide 5: Why Hedera

Keep this practical, not generic.

- HCS gives ordered, low-cost proof and treasury checkpoints
- HTS-style receipts represent impact-linked financial actions
- low fees make repeated milestone and verification updates feasible
- Hedera fits sustainability because the workflow depends on many small, auditable state changes

Show one real HCS transaction ID and one real token result from the current build.

## Slide 6: Why This Can Deploy

Buyer:

- CSR teams
- adaptation funds
- municipal resilience programs

Operator:

- NGOs
- cooperatives
- local project delivery partners

Verifier:

- field auditors
- satellite / IoT data partners
- environmental review organizations

Message:

`We are not asking the market to adopt a new abstract dashboard. We are replacing a broken payout workflow.`

## Slide 7: Demo Proof

Show one flagship case only.

Recommended order:

1. project enters with proof packet
2. Case Room shows sponsor, verifier, and operator sync
3. Operations shows payout recommendation and milestone gates
4. Client Portal shows release-ready alert
5. Audit Trail shows Hedera-backed event line

## Slide 8: Roadmap

Next 90 days:

- pilot one wedge in one geography
- onboard one verifier workflow
- add sponsor approval and recurring treasury deposit flow
- add production proof upload and policy checks

Next 6 months:

- launch first live funded case
- add partner reporting
- expand from one wedge to adjacent categories

## Slide 9: Ask

`We want to pilot EcoSwarm Regen with one climate operator, one verifier, and one sponsor class to prove faster trusted payouts for real community climate work.`

If speaking to incubator-style judges:

`What we need is pilot access, verifier partnerships, and deployment design partners.`
